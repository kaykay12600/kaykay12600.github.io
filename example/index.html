More than ever, we are using our eyes for everyday tasks. We
use our eyes to stare at small texts whether we notice it or not
due to television screens, computer monitors, and mobile
devices. All these lead to eye fatigue as well as.
age related problems within our eyes.

It has been recently reported that Americans more than
ever have been facing the threat of blindness from age-
related eye disease. Over one million Americans ages
40 and over are currently blind and an additional 2.4
million are visually impaired. These numbers are expected
to double over the next 30 years as the Baby Boomer
generation from the early 2000s gets older.
